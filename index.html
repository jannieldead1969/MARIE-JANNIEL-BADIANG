Creative Freedom.
Welcome to my page. I am Marie Janniel H. Badiang, 20 years old, from Brgy. V, Tangub City, Misamis Occidental.
My life’s goal is to create an art not bounded by rules and not restrained by traditional mentality. I open my mind to newer versions of how to represent emotions and messages, breaking fourth walls, and branding them for you if you hold my designs deep in your heart. My creation does not represent brutality but the beauty amidst the chaos. I am a person who enjoys lightning and thunderstorms, horror movies, and crime documentaries. I am not afraid to use darkness for light to shine through. Therefore, welcome to my personal website. I will show you my posters which are a product of true creative freedom at its finest in the form of Graphic Designs.
